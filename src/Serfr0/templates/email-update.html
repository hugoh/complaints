Hello !

(You're getting this email because this address has been registered on the website http://complaints.serfr1.org, and I have a few updates I'd like to share with all the users of that site.)

First: please re-enter your address !

The address field in http://complaints.serfr1.org/profile has been upgraded; just start typing your address, and it should auto-complete just like Google maps. Click on the correct address, and it will also regenerate your lat/long.

It only takes a few seconds, but it's a huge help in helping me generate structured address info, which is what I need for the all-new non-email submittions system ... more below.

A Wall of Shame: http://stop.jetnoise.net/report/yesterday

This list is compiled from all of your reports from the day before, and gives a good summary of which planes and airlines you found most (and least !) annoying.

Working with sfo.noise@flysfo.com.

The volume of the emails we send has caused them some indigestion. I've spent the past month working with them, resending and reformatting all the complaints to make sure they will include them in their reports. No complaints have been lost (they were all in the database anyway), and things will run more smoothly in the future.

We're moving to a new system that doesn't involve email - but requires accurate address information or the reports won't get accepted. So again, please check your profile, reenter your address, and let Google maps auto-complete it.

Rebranding: complaints are now reports

A lot of people are put off 'complaining', as they don't like to think of themselves as 'complainers'. So now we are reporters of disturbances.

A new name: http://stop.jetnoise.net/

The old name confused some people, who thought it was specific to SERFR1. In fact it works for anyone around SFO, under any of the flightpaths. So there is a new name, to make that clear.

The old name will continue to work alongside the new one; both will work indefinitely, and it doesn't matter at all which you use, they both point to the same site. But I'll use the new name in announcements etc.

A new getting started guide: http://stop.jetnoise.net/intro

Want to tell your neighbors how to report disturbances ? Just give them that URL.

Does it matter if a flight is not identified ? No.

I'd just like to reassure people: it really doesn't matter if the site can't identify a particular aircraft. The SFO office doesn't do much with that information anyway, and they will still record your complaint (erm, report :) anyway. After all, most people can't identify flights when they phone in complaints.

I think identifying the flight makes the whole process more engaging, and makes it more likely people will click on 'REPORT IT' - and we need lots of clicks (and more importantly, lots more people clicking). So I'll keep trying to make the flight identification work better for people, and fix it when it breaks. But it's basically for our own consumption, and has no impact on what happens to the reports/complaints we send in.

So don't worry if the flight is not identified - your report/complaint will still count.

Finally: I will be mostly offline for a while

I'm travelling for the next 4-5 weeks, and mostly unable to do anything except read/write email. If something bad happens, apologies in advance - I won't be able to fix it until I get back :( But that's not too likely - the site's been running pretty well over the past month.
