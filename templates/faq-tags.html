Flight tags

SERFR1: a flight that flew in on SERFR1.
BRIXX: a flight that flew in on BRIXX1.
ADSB: flights that have locally received track data (ADS-B).
FA:TA: flights that have FlightAware track data (source: ADS-B).
FA:TZ: flights that have FlightAware track data (source: radar).
ClassB:ADSB: violations detected using locally received ADS-B data. Very usable.
ClassB:FA: violations detected using FlightAware data. Usable.
